About Team-8A:

Our business involves providing you with suggestions on the best movies according to your personal taste, and showing you where you can view them.

What we do: In order to customize your experiences, we collect, process and storage your interactions with our website and app. In general, all of your data is collected in an anonymized and pseudonymized form, and we may collect your personal data, however, for explicitly defined applications. Subject to your consent, we process some data with the help of third parties. Please read further to obtain additional details on this.

What we do not do: We never collect sensitive information such as financial or health data. We never create profiles with legal ramifications pursuant to Article 22 of the GDPR. We never sell information on the user level (e.g. "Which movie did a particular user click on?"), unless this occurs in an aggregated form ("Which movies are popular among users?").

About your data:

We collect data from you and about you.

Why we collect data.

We use tracking technologies for many reasons:

To gain a better understanding of our audience, our customers, our website visitors and their respective interests.

To identify new visitors to our websites and apps, in addition to previous customers.

To create a profile based on your taste in movies, by means of which we are able to provide you with recommendations responding to your personal taste in movies and your consumer preferences.

To send you customized or interest-based advertising optimized for your profile. These ads may be displayed both on JustWatch and on other websites you visit (with help of retargeting technology).

Types of data we collect

Contact information. For example, we may collect your e-mail address if you register via e-mail for an account or to receive our e-mails or to provide feedback on our forms.

Information that you transmit or post publicly. For example, we collect information that you post in a public area on our website. We also collect information if you take part in a survey.

Demographic information. We may collect your zip code in order to assist you in finding movie theaters relevant to you.

We passively collect information on your browsing history on JustWatch. We use unique browser cookies and web beacons in order to identify your browser by pseudonym.

We may receive a limited amount of information on you from third parties. Examples of this are your profile ID and information to which you have allowed access via a particular registration provider during registration. From some partner tracking networks, we may also receive information on entertainment purchases that you have made via a link originating from JustWatch.

Other information. If you use our website, we may also collect information on your IP address and the browser you are using. We may look at the site from where you came or which websites you visit when you leave us. If you use our mobile app, we may identify your GPS location, subject to your additional consent. We might look at how often you use the app and where you downloaded it.

How we store and process your data

We process and store your data in high security data centers within the EU, operated by Amazon Web Services and the Google Cloud Platform. These data centers are secured and certified according to the highest standards (minimum ISO 27001, 27017, 27018, EU Privacy Shield and standard contractual clauses) and protected through signed data protection addenda.

All information provided is encrypted both during transmission (TLS) and at rest. Employee access to personal information is limited and validated.

The processing and storage of information that is possibly personally identifiable is kept to a minimum required for operating the service (for example: your mobile GPS location is rounded off to the nearest 100 meters before our systems are requested to find movie theaters near you). Where required, this type of information is subject to access control, strict retention and pseudonymization.

We retain personal data as long as is required or relevant for the practices described in this Policy, however no longer than for a total of 50 months. After this period, all your data is automatically erased if you are no longer active on JustWatch, unless retention of your data is mandated by law.

We retain your data with the highest standard of currently available security processes and technologies; the Internet itself, however, is not 100% secure. We cannot promise that your use of our websites will be completely secure. We recommend that you exercise caution in the use of the Internet. This includes never sharing your passwords with others.
